Hippo

Hippopotamuses give bird in the water.

  • Scientific Name: Hippopotamus
  • Average Lifespan: 45 years
  • Average Speed: 30 km/h
  • Habitat: rivers, lakes, swamps

Measured around 3.5m long and 1.5m tall, and can weigh up 3,200kg, hippos are considered as the third largest land animal. They are great swimmers and can hold their breath for up to five minutes underwater.
